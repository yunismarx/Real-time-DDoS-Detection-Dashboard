THE SOLUTION

A prototype IDS that integrates advanced Machine Learning (XGBoost) and Deep Learning (DNN) algorithms with Big Data analytics to detect anomalies that rule-based systems miss.

METHODOLOGY & ENGINEERING

Preventing Bias & Data Leakage through Rigorous Preprocessing

01

Data Cleaning

Replacing infinite values (+∞, -∞) and handling null entries to prevent mathematical instability in the models.

02

Deduplication

Removing duplicate records to prevent "Data Leakage," ensuring the model learns underlying patterns rather than memorizing repeated rows.

03

Robust Scaling

Applying statistical scaling (Median & IQR) to handle Outliers common in "bursty" DDoS attacks, protecting the model from noise.

Result: High-fidelity datasets ready for training without bias.
